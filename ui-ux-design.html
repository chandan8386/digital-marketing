Building Blocks of UX Design Services

Understanding the target audience is the first step. This involves conducting user research, including surveys, interviews, and observation, to gain insights into user behaviors, needs, and pain points.

Creating user personas based on research helps define archetypical users and their characteristics. This allows designers to design with specific user needs in mind.

Structuring content and information logically is crucial. Information architecture involves organizing content to make it easily accessible and navigable.

Before diving into full design, wireframes and prototypes are created to plan the layout, flow, and interaction design of the product or website.
